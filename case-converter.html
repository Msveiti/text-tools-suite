About Case Converter

Convert Text Case Online

Our free case converter tool allows you to quickly change the capitalization of your text. Whether you need to convert text to uppercase, lowercase, title case, or sentence case, our tool makes it simple and instant.

Available Conversions

  • UPPERCASE: Converts all letters to capital letters
  • lowercase: Converts all letters to small letters
  • Title Case: Capitalizes the first letter of each major word
  • Sentence case: Capitalizes only the first letter of each sentence
  • Capitalize Each Word: Capitalizes the first letter of every word
  • aLtErNaTiNg CaSe: Alternates between lowercase and uppercase
  • iNVERSE cASE: Swaps uppercase and lowercase letters
