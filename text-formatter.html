What is a Text Formatter?

A text formatter is a smart tool that automatically adds punctuation, fixes capitalization, and structures your text properly. Perfect for:

  • Social media posts: Quickly format text for Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn
  • Quick messages: Clean up rushed typing before sending
  • Voice-to-text cleanup: Fix dictation errors and add punctuation
  • Non-native speakers: Ensure proper English grammar and punctuation
  • Content drafts: Format rough ideas into readable text
  • Professional communication: Polish casual text for business use

Example: Before & After

Before (Raw Text):

hey everyone just wanted to share some exciting news we finally launched our new product after months of hard work im so grateful to the team for their dedication cant wait to hear what you all think check it out and let me know your thoughts

After (Formatted):

Hey everyone! Just wanted to share some exciting news. We finally launched our new product after months of hard work. I'm so grateful to the team for their dedication. Can't wait to hear what you all think! Check it out and let me know your thoughts.
